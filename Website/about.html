About Us
About Us

The Western Blue Tongues are a team at Southern River College, founded in 2023 to inspire creativity to the students at Southern River College. Despite being a fairly new team, our mentors and leaders have been part of the FRC since 2016.

At WBT, we strive to create a competition-ready robot before the deadline.

Our Mission

The mission of FIRST is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
